FAQ


Q.

If I've never used a Classroom Response System, how do I choose between the two options?

If you previously used the iClicker remotes, you may want to start with REEF/Cloud, since the interface will be familiar.

You may also want to check with a few of you colleagues, and see which they prefer, and why.

 


Q.

Will my students need to pay these tools?

No. Both tools have been licensed institutionally, and can be used free of charge by faculty and students. 

 


Q.

If a student has already purchased a clicker remote, will they be able to use it during the Fall semseter?

The bookstore will buy back clicker remotes for $10. Clicker remotes are not supported by either of these two tools. 

 


Q.

I have an old reciever unit from when I used iClicker. What should I do with it?

You may bring it to the FTC in MCK355. If you have multpile receiver units in your department, give them to your department Office Assistant, and we will come and pick them up.
